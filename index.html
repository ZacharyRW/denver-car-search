💡 Buying tips for this budget

  • Get a pre-purchase inspection (~$100–150 at any indie shop) before buying anything at this age/mileage.
  • Hybrids: ask about traction-battery health and warning lights. Toyota hybrid batteries commonly last 200k+; Honda Civic Hybrid (IMA) batteries are the weak point.
  • Used EVs (Leaf): check battery "bars." A 2011–2013 Leaf may show only ~40–70 mi real range, and Colorado winters cut EV range ~30%. 2017+ Leaf is safer for year-round.
  • Chevy Volt (plug-in): ~35–53 mi electric covers most daily driving on your home outlet, then gas with no range anxiety.
  • Child safety: confirm working LATCH anchors; check the model's IIHS rating.
  • Run a VIN title/history check and avoid salvage/rebuilt titles.
